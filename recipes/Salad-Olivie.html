"Olivier Salad" recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 sour pickles
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 slices good quality ham
  • 2 potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • Mayonnaise

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes, carrots, & eggs
  2. Chop all the ingredients, both boiled and raw, into fairly uniform small cubes (except for the peas)
  3. Mix all the ingredients together well. Add the mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. You can vary the quantities based on personal preference. Make sure you balance them out well, like in this recipe, i.e., if you want a double amount, double all the ingredients listed in this recipe.
